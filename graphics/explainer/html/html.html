A year of late school buses

This is a story about buses. Boston Public School buses.
On any given school day, hundreds of buses carry thousands of students to school. Here's Sept. 4, 2013, the first day of classes: 1,140 bus trips.
And here’s the rest of the year — an average of 1,380 school bus trips per day for the 2013-14 academic year.
More than 14% of the buses failed to arrive at least five minutes before the opening bell, when the school district considered the buses contractually late.
Buses came in late every day, some trips later than others. Over the course of the year those late minutes added up to hours of lost instruction for students.

SOURCE: Boston Public Schools
