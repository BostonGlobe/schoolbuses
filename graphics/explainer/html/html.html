This is a story about buses. Boston Public School buses.
On any given day, hundreds of buses carry thousands of kids to school. Here’s September 4, 2013: the first day of classes.
And here's the rest of the year — an average of 1,380 bus trips per day.
Over 14% of buses fail to arrive at least five minutes before the opening bell, which the school district counts as late.
Over the school year, as drivers become familiar with their routes, tardiness decreases. But buses continue to arrive late.
